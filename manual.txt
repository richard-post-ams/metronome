How to try it:

Start/Stop
Click Start to begin the metronome.
Adjust BPM using the dial (drag around, or scroll).

Mixer
Play with Quarter / 8th / 16th / Triplet sliders to hear each layer.

Speed Trainer
Enable it, set step, every-bars, and max; let it ramp up as you play.

Gap Click
Enable, choose A/B bars, and feel the silent bars testing your internal pulse.

Timing Analyzer
Switch to Timing Analyzer tab.
Start the metronome.

Use On-screen Hit: tap the Hit button in time; watch offsets.
Try Microphone: switch to Mic, grant permission, play/clap; adjust threshold to ignore room noise.
